about

By emily
January 10, 2026

welcome! this is a database of helpful resources for autistic people. it's run by [two autistic people, emily & jay], along with our resident autistic alien, aylin.*

these resources are meant to be inclusive, neurodivergence-affirming, and not a futile attempt to magically convert us into neurotypical folk. ideally, everything is by the autistic community, for the autistic community here.

lastly, thank you for being here. i hope these resources help show you that you're not alone. <3
